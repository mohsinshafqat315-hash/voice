## Introduction

LedgerSmart AI ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our receipt and invoice management service.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Email address
- Name
- Company name
- Country and currency preferences
- Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)

### Receipt Data
- Uploaded receipt images and PDFs
- Extracted data from receipts (vendor, amount, date, etc.)
- OCR processing results
- AI analysis results

### Usage Data
- Login timestamps
- API usage
- Feature usage statistics

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain our service
- To process payments and manage subscriptions
- To improve our AI and OCR accuracy
- To send important service updates
- To comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely in MongoDB Atlas
- Receipt files are stored in secure cloud storage
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
- All API communications use HTTPS
- JWT tokens for secure authentication

## Third-Party Services

- **Stripe**: Payment processing (see Stripe Privacy Policy)
- **MongoDB Atlas**: Database hosting
- **OCR Providers**: Receipt text extraction
